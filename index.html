The Algerian Research Hub is a project started in October 2021 to improve the research capacity and collaboration between Algeria and North American researchers in certain field. Research efforts seek to develop research in the hopes of benefiting the Algerian economy and society, in general. The purpose of this site is to enable information sharing, and research best practices.
